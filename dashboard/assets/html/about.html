About Mental Health Tracker
üëã Welcome to the Mental Cares Dashboard! This dashboard is a tool that tracks the prevalence and patterns of mental health illness among international students, and provides with mental health care services on campus and around the United States. It allows users to:
  • Track and compare the depression prevalence and utilization rate of mental health services among international students by origin
  • Explore current mental care resources availability on campus and nearby
  • Make an appointment with a mental health counselor or for a visit to mental health sites
    • This dashboard is not intended to be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
    Data Source
    The data underlying this dashboard is compiled from administrative health care claims datasets:
  • USC Counseling & Mental Health Services
  • National Survey on Drug Use and Health
  • Open Doors
    •  Primarily, this dashboard is designed for institutional use, and some charts only represent sample data without verification. The dashboard will be updated periodically as new data becomes available.
